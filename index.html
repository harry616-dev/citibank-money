FAQs

How do I regain access to my account online? ▼

There are a few ways to regain online access to your account:

  • Open the Citi Mobile App on your mobile device and use the QR code to sign into Citi.com.
  • Choose Forgot User ID or Password on Citi.com and follow the instructions.
  • If you need guidance, select Chat and we'll walk you through it.
How do I activate a card? ▼

You can activate your card through several methods:

  • Call the activation phone number on the sticker attached to your card
  • Log into your Citi account online and activate through your account dashboard
  • Use the Citi Mobile App to activate your card
How do I manage a dispute on my account? ▼

To manage a dispute on your account:

  • Sign into your account and go to the Account Summary page
  • Select the account with the disputed transaction
  • Find the transaction and select "Dispute Transaction"
  • Follow the prompts to submit your dispute
What do I need to apply for a Citibank account online? ▼

To apply for a Citibank account online, you'll need:

  • Social Security Number
  • Valid government-issued ID
  • Proof of address (utility bill, lease agreement, etc.)
  • Initial deposit amount (varies by account type)
  • Contact information including phone number and email
